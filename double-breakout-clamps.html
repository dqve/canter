Double Breakout Casing ClamPs

Double Breakout Casing Clamps.

A max 8.8" ID for casings and min 2.5" ID for drill rods. The Double Breakout Clamps come with a top hydraulic breakout cylinder set of jaws and a bottom set of fixed grip jaws. The grip jaws have hardened steel grip 'teeth' to securely hold casings and rods. Capable of gripping of gripping and breaking out even the toughest casings. The Double Breakout Clamps are available on the following machines:

  • K40 Drilling Rig.
  • K60 Drilling Rig.
  • K2 & K3 Mast Attachments.
