Hydraulic Breakout ClamPs

Standard Clam Clamps and Hydraulic Breakout Wrench.

A max 9" ID for casings and min 1" ID for drill rods. The Clam Clamps come with a hydraulic breakout cylinder and two rod wrenches sized to the customers drill rods. The rods guides or 'slips' are also sized to fit the drill rods or casings as required. Capable of gripping both steel and PVC casings the Clam Clamp come as standard on the following machines:

  • K40 Drilling Rig.
  • K60 Drilling Rig.
  • K2 & K3 Mast Attachments.
