Equipment Overview

K60 Drill Rig

The K60 uses 10ft drill rods, with onboard power and rod rack. The many included options make the K60, which utilizes the K3 mast, a versatile and powerful drilling unit for various applications, capable of mud and air drilling.

K40 Drill Rig

An extrememly powerful rig for its size, with many available options and configurations. Utilizing the K2 mast the K40 is capable of mud and air drilling for geothermal, foundation and geotechnical drilling.

Full View

K40 Short Mast

The short mast K40 is ideal for lowheadroom, micro-piling and limited access drilling applications, foundation, underpinning and underground geothermal drilling. Has drilled over 1,000 ft deep.

Full View

R30 DRILL Rig

A lower cost option drilling unit with a compact track base for easy transportation and site access. The R30 utilizes the K1 drill mast and is capable of mud and air drilling with available drill tooling.

Full View

K1 Drill Mast

The K1 uses 10ft drill rods and easily attaches to compact excavators and skid steers for shallow and residential drilling applications.

Full View

K2 Drill Mast

The K2 attachment utilizes 5ft drill rods for geothermal and foundation drilling applications. With high torque and deep drilling cabilities the K2 is ideal for excavator/truck mounting.

K3 Drill Mast

The K3 uses a max 10ft drill rod with optional setups for augers and drill rods. The high torque JT204 offers up to 10,000 ft.lbs of torque and 2-drill speeds.
