R10 Man Portable Drill

All-In-One Drilling Rig for small bore, air & mud drilling projects.

The R10 Mini-Tech has available setups for auger, mud or air drilling. These compact units are compatible with all drilling methods currently available. This lightweight unit is man & helicopter portable with low impact, turf friendly tires. These cost effective drill units provide flexibility and power, while maintaining portability and convenience.

  • Saftey Clamp System
