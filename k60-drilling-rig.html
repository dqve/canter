K60 Drilling RIg

Geothermal & Geotechnical Drill - Mud & Air Drilling Setups.

The K60 is a powerful and versatile drilling rig with setups for gethermal, geotechnical and water well drilling applications. High torque and high speed drill head options are available. The K60 is a capable geothermal drilling rig for residential and commercial geothermal projects.
