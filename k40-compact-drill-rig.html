K40 Geothermal Drilling Rig

Limited Access - Geothermal - Geotechnical & Micro Piling.

The K40 is a compact drilling unit for limited access drilling sites for geothermal and micro piling applications. Geothermal setups for mud or air drilling available. Standard features include breakout clamps, separate power unit, width adjustable crawler tracks, adjustable jack legs and an auxiliary control lever.

  • JT200 Series Drill Heads
  • Saftey Clamp System
  • Mud Drilling Options
