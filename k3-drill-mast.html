Excavator Drill Attachment

K3 Excavator Drill Mast Attachment

The K3 Excavator Drill Mast Attachment uses 10 ft drill rods with the high torque JT200 Series drill heads for geothermal, geotechnical and foundation applications. With setups for mud, air and geotechnical drilling this excavator mounted drill is a powerful drill attachment for commercial drilling projects. Setups available for 24" OD bore holes with up to 12" DTH hammers or augers.

  • JT200 Series Drill Heads
  • Saftey Clamp System
  • Grout Mixers
