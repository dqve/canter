Drilling RIgs - R30 All-in-one

All-In-One Drilling Rig for small bore, air & mud drilling projects.

The R30 is a compact drilling rig for mud or air drilling. The high speed drill head and optional side port swivel make it ideal for mud drilling applications or DTH hammer setups. The R30 is the most cost effective all-in-one drilling unit for entry into the geothermal, water well and geotechnical markets.
