remote control Drillling

Wireless Remote Controls for tracks, feed and drilling functions.

Available with a maximum 1,500 ft wireless range, this compact transmitter can be configured with toggle switches, micro-joysticks or sliders as drill options may require. With a battery life of up to 16 hours. Compact for hand-held operation, includes waist and neck straps and is environmentally sealed for harsh site conditions. Available for the following machines:

  • K40 Drilling Rig.
  • K60 Drilling Rig.
  • Mast Attachments.
