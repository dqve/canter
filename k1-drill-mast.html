Skid Steer Drill Attachment

K1 Drill Mast Attachment for Skid Steers & Mini Excavators

The K1 Drill Mast Attachment is a skid steer drill attachment and is also available as an excavator drill attachment. Capable of drilling with augers, helical anchors, ground screws, DTH hammers and mud drilling setups the K1 can drill over 200 ft for small bore applications, or to shallower depths for larger bore holes, i.e foundation and underpinning applications.

  • JT200 Series Drill Heads
  • Saftey Clamp System
  • Grout Mixers
