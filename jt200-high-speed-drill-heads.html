Drill Heads - JT200-M Series

High Speed Drill Heads for Mud, Air and Auger Drilling.

The JT200-M Series Rotation Heads have 2 set speeds with variable speed control in between. The high torque/low speed and low torque/high speed settings allow the operator to alternate the drill head's power and speed for different applications and drill methods. A 2-3/8" center bore allows for mud or air flushing with the optional 2,500 PSI 90 degree swivel.

  • 2,500 PSI 90 Degree Swivel.
  • Drill Rod Adaptor - all sizes available.
  • Saver-Sub Adaptor - for above.
